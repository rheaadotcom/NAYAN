# 🧠 Project NAYAN

> **AI-Powered Early Learning Assessment Platform**

## 🌟 Vision

Project NAYAN aims to empower teachers and parents by providing an AI-powered platform for the **early screening of learning difficulties** in children through interactive assessments.

The platform helps identify children who may require professional evaluation, enabling timely intervention and personalized learning support.

> ⚠️ **Disclaimer:** Project NAYAN is an early screening tool. It does **not** diagnose medical conditions. Professional evaluation is always recommended.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

Millions of children struggle with learning difficulties such as dyslexia, dysgraphia, ADHD, and dyscalculia. Due to the lack of affordable screening tools and trained specialists, these challenges often remain undetected until they significantly affect academic performance and confidence.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

Project NAYAN combines:

- 📝 Handwriting Assessment
- 📖 Reading Assessment
- 🎮 Cognitive Games
- 🎤 Speech Assessment *(Future)*
- 👀 Eye Tracking *(Future)*

Using AI, the platform generates explainable risk reports and personalized recommendations to support teachers and parents.
